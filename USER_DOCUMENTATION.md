# MR.CREAMS User Guide

## Introduction

Welcome to MR.CREAMS - the Conflict Resolution and Emotional Analysis Management System. This platform helps couples identify, understand, and resolve relationship conflicts through AI-powered insights and professional guidance.

## Getting Started

### Account Creation

1. Visit the MR.CREAMS website
2. Click "Create Account" on the login page
3. Fill in your details and select your role (Individual/Couple)
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile information

### Logging In

1. Visit the MR.CREAMS website
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In as User" (or "Sign In as Professional" if applicable)

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides a quick overview of your relationship insights:

- **Harmony Opportunities**: Number of conflicts recorded
- **Emotional Awareness**: Average emotional intensity
- **Harmony Recovery**: Average resolution time
- **Growth Opportunity**: Most common conflict reason
- **Recent Harmony Opportunities**: List of recent conflicts
- **Quick Tips**: Helpful relationship advice

## Key Features

### Sharing Challenges

1. Click "Share Challenge" on the dashboard
2. Describe the conflict situation
3. Rate the emotional intensity
4. Select the primary reason
5. Add any additional context
6. Submit to receive AI-powered insights

### Emotional Check-In

Use the Emotion Check-In widget to:
1. Record your current emotional state
2. Track emotional patterns over time
3. Receive personalized recommendations

### Harmony Hub

The Harmony Hub shows all your recorded conflicts and their resolutions:
1. Navigate to "Harmony Hub" from the main menu
2. View conflicts sorted by date
3. Click on any conflict to see details and recommendations
4. Track progress on resolution strategies

### Support System

If you need assistance:
1. Click the floating support button (bottom-right corner)
2. Fill in the ticket details
3. Submit your request
4. Track the status in the "My Tickets" section

## Professional Features

### For Therapists

- **Client Management**: View and manage your client list
- **Session Scheduling**: Schedule and manage therapy sessions
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor client progress and outcomes
- **Resource Library**: Access therapeutic resources and exercises

### For Administrators

- **User Management**: Add, edit, and manage user accounts
- **Organization Settings**: Configure organization-specific settings
- **Analytics Dashboard**: View system usage and outcome metrics
- **Support Management**: Handle support tickets and inquiries

## Mobile Access

MR.CREAMS is available as a Progressive Web App (PWA):
1. Visit the website on your mobile device
2. Add to home screen when prompted
3. Access all features offline
4. Receive notifications for important updates

## Privacy & Security

- All data is encrypted and securely stored
- Information is never shared with third parties
- You control what information is visible to therapists
- Two-factor authentication available for added security

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

- **Login Problems**: Reset your password or contact support
- **Data Not Saving**: Check your internet connection
- **App Not Loading**: Clear browser cache or try a different browser
- **Missing Features**: Ensure your account has the correct permissions
